Title: ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks  
Authors: Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, Geoffrey Hinton  
Year: 2012  
Journal/Conference: Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS)  

Summary: 
This paper introduced deep convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for image classification and achieved breakthrough performance on the ImageNet dataset, marking the rise of deep learning in computer vision.
